Team

From that humble beginning in the library, we’re now bringing Map It Projects to companies everywhere, passionate about simplifying the complex and helping organizations map their path to success.

James Lloyd

Managing Director

James Lloyd is the MD of Map IT Projects LTD. He has years of experience in digital projects ranging from system training to system implementation, data visualisations, and more. He has experience in project management, process mapping, data visualisation (Power BI), and database building.

He is backed up with APM, Microsoft, and Idea Awards accreditations. This ranges from the APM Project Management Qualification (APM PMQ) and Microsoft Office Specialist Qualification (MOS) in MS Access. With this experience he is determined to deliver quality work tailored to the customers' needs, aiming for project success and satisfied clients. With a mixture of experience and knowledge, he will be the team leader across the business to strive for success and ensure standards are met.

Tony Osomade

Chief Financial Officer

Tony Osomade, a strategic finance leader and certified project management professional, with an MBA and a first-class BSc in Digital Business is set to drive MAP IT PROJECTS LTD's growth in process mapping. As a Fellow of the ACCA and a member of the CPA Ireland, he combines financial governance with technological innovation, essential for scaling process mapping solutions.

Tony is poised to leverage his digital transformation expertise and experience in system implementation to enhance the company's market position. Previously at Tritek Consulting Ltd, he led enterprise-wide system migrations and implemented financial controls, uniquely positioning him to oversee both fiscal and operational growth.

Khalifa Tagud

Chief Sales and Marketing Officer

Khalifa’s portfolio consists of experience with international conglomerates, including SAP and Al Jazeera Media Network, where he has gained in-depth knowledge of business processes and driving them through technology. In previous roles, he demonstrated a strong capability for designing and executing successful client engagements, with focus on facilitating Presales and Sales activities that address the needs of both mid-market and large enterprise clients.

Certified in SAP Analytics Cloud, he is committed to leveraging data for strategic insights. With a proven track record of developing innovative marketing strategies, Khalifa consistently enhances customer experience, enabling organizations to build lasting relationships with their clients. At Map It, he will lead Sales and Marketing efforts, bringing his unique combination of skills to the forefront to drive client success.
